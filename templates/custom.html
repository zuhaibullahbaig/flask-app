What are custom backlinks?

Well when you shorten a link with our url shortener, you may have seen that the backlink after the domain consists of 6 random ascii characters. Maybe you want to use your own backlink that represents your company and your link pretty well. A link like https://chotuurl.com/your-brand-name.

Reaaally?

Yeah, with ChotuURL being a go-to source for people, we don't charge any money; you can create custom backlinks for free. But, hold on, there is a limit: your custom links will expire in 30 days. but Its a long time isn't it. And for thirty days we will be giving you link clicks and other statistics. its so good for things like forms, marketing campaigns and other things that stay for under a month. If we are hiring people for ChotuURL, and the process lasts for 21 days, we will shorten a link which goes like https://chotuurl.com/chotu_form..
